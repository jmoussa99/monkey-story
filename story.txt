One day, Marwan the spidermonkey was chilling in the canopies of Brazil eating some fruit.
Marwan's brother, Karim decided to join Marwan and started eating fruit of his own.
Marwan got angry because he really likes fruits and doesn't want to share.
Karim then convinces Marwan that its better to share because it ensures their survival.
Suddenly a wild cheetah decides to attack the spidermonkey brothers...
